Ideal Job

The ideal job I have chosen for myself is a ‘Full Stack Developer,’ working on exciting start-ups and projects with clients. A Full Stack developer works on both the front-end and the backend of the business. The role of being a Full Stack developer somewhat appeals to me because their job encompasses everything from the web design to databases etc. I would get more experience partnering with clients building their start-ups, see the development of SaaS companies, and eventually make a start-up developing my own apps. Another bonus is that you can spend more time with your family by working at home and that the hours are flexible.

The company requires skills like PHP, React, Redux, and REST APIs. You would also need experience in visual design, having a solid understanding of UI and UX, as well as strong communication skills.

I am quite young, so I do not currently have a lot of life experience, but I do learn quickly and take passion in learning. I hold many certifications related to the mining industry, such as rope access, confined spaces, gas testing, standard 11, etc. as well as being qualified to be an NDT tech (ISO 9712). With the experience I have, working at the mines (six months), I’ve been bombarded continuously with team-building exercises, safety talks, and taking pride in one’s work due to the work culture we have at my workplace. As a hobby, I like to build online stores and advertise them using FB Ads, learning many lessons such as persistence, business ethics, and the importance of excellent customer service.

To get the position, you must start small, building your experience, your portfolio, and your skills. Finish the Bachelor of IT, do some freelances, and start some personal projects that I love. Get comfortable with different coding languages and learn some design principles as well. I’d especially take a course in public speaking or body language lessons to better my pitch.
